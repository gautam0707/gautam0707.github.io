I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security, Saarbrücken, Germany. My research is jointly supervised by [Dr. Rebekka Burkholz](https://sites.google.com/view/rebekkaburkholz/) and [Dr. Krikamol Muandet](https://www.krikamol.org/). My research focuses on causality inspired machine learning, explainability, and statistical causal inference. <strong style="color:#c00;">I am currently seeking research positions in both academia and industry</strong>.

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, where I was advised by [Prof. Vineeth N Balasubramanian](https://people.iith.ac.in/vineethnb/index.html). During my Ph.D., I was awarded the prestigious Prime Minister’s Research Fellowship (PMRF).
